# Product Principles

PropAI connects property seekers with real brokers through WhatsApp — the channel where Indian real estate actually operates.

## What we solve

Indian real estate runs on WhatsApp broker groups. Listings spread through forwarded messages, voice notes, and screenshots. There is no single source of truth. Buyers miss fresh listings. Brokers repeat themselves. Data goes stale within hours.

PropAI captures this stream, structures it, and makes it searchable — so buyers find real inventory and brokers reach serious inquiries.

## Non-negotiables

1. **Never fabricate inventory.** Every listing traces back to a real WhatsApp message from a real broker. No synthetic data, no "sample" listings, no placeholders pretending to be properties.

2. **Freshness > quantity.** A 2-hour-old listing beats a 2-month-old one. We hide stale listings after 30 days of inactivity. We never pad counts with dead inventory.

3. **Broker owns the relationship.** PropAI is a discovery layer, not a middleman. Enquiries go directly to the broker on WhatsApp. We never insert ourselves between buyer and broker.

4. **WhatsApp is the source of truth.** If a broker didn't say it in a WhatsApp group, it's not in PropAI. We don't accept manual data entry that can't be traced to a message.

5. **Explainability over black-box AI.** Every parsed listing shows its source message. Every AI extraction includes confidence signals. Users and brokers can always verify.

## Success metric

A property seeker finds a fresh, relevant listing and contacts the broker directly on WhatsApp — within 5 minutes of opening PropAI.

Secondary: brokers receive qualified enquiries (not spam) and close deals faster than through portal leads.

## What we are NOT

- Not a property portal (we don't accept paid listings).
- Not a CRM (we don't manage broker pipelines).
- Not a chatbot (we connect humans to humans).
- Not a data aggregator (we capture one network's live signal, not scrape the web).
